Our Story

Founded in 2000 (Ethiopian calendar) by technology expert Eyob Asefa, TE Computer Solution started in Addis Ababa, Sarbet, Ethiopia. What began as a passionate initiative has grown into a trusted name in professional computer maintenance and IT support.

Over the years, we’ve served a wide range of businesses — including engineering firms, financial offices, restaurants, and manufacturing companies. Our deep understanding of how essential computers are in the modern workspace allows us to deliver tailored services that ensure your technology runs smoothly, efficiently, and without interruption.

We know what it means when your computer isn’t office-friendly — that’s why we stay ahead with up-to-date skills, advanced diagnostics, and customer-first service. At TE Computer Solution, we don't just fix issues; we help businesses thrive through reliable technology support.

We are committed to continuous improvement, investing in training and tools that keep us at the cutting edge of the IT support industry — because your success depends on technology that works as hard as you do.
